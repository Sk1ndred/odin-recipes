Ginger and Chilli Salmon

For a quick and healthy meal that is a firm favourite, try this weeknight delight. The amount of chilli is only a guide and can be changed to the amount of heat that you fancy. This dish is fantastic served with rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 Salmon fillets
  • 1 thumb sized piece on ginger
  • 2 teaspoons of chilli flakes
  • The juice of 1 whole lemon
  • 50 ml of soy sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C
  2. Place the 2 salmon fillets in a small over proof dish that is about the same size as the fillets laying flat.
  3. Add the juice of the lemon and the soy sauce over and around the salon fillets.
  4. Finely chop the ginger and place this on top of the fillets.
  5. Sprinkle the chilli flakes over each of the 2 fillets.
  6. Cover the fillets with the lid and cook in the oven for about 20 minutes

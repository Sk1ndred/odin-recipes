Breakfast Eggs

I stole the basis of this recipe off of Nigella. I added some meat to help to cure that hangover itch. Don't forget to have some good bread to mop up the yummy tomato sauce at the end.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove of garlic, diced
  • 130g pack of pancetta
  • 1 heaped teaspoon of chilli flakes
  • 1 tin of tomato sauce
  • 4 eggs
  • 10g of grated parmigiano reggiano

Steps

  1. Place a pan on a medium heat hob and heat the olive oil.
  2. Cook the pancetta until it's almost done and then add the diced ginger.
  3. Once the pancetta and ginger are cooked, add the chilli flakes and mix them around in the oil.
  4. Tip in the chopped tomatoes and ensure that they are bubbling.
  5. Crack the 4 eggs into the sauce, sprinkle on the cheese and cover.
  6. Cook until the eggs are poached to your liking and serve with a hunk of bread.
